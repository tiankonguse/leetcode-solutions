# Sudoku Solver 

Write a program to solve a Sudoku puzzle by filling the empty cells.  

Empty cells are indicated by the character `'.'`.  


You may assume that there will be only one unique solution.  

```
 5 3 . | . 7 . | . . . |
 6 . . | 1 9 5 | . . . |
 . 9 8 | . . . | . 6 . |
 - - -   - - -   - - -  
 8 . . | . 6 . | . . 3 |
 4 . . | 8 . 3 | . . 1 |
 7 . . | . 2 . | . . 6 |
 - - -   - - -   - - -  
 . 6 . | . . . | 2 8 . |
 . . . | 4 1 9 | . . 5 |
 . . . | . 8 . | . 7 9 |
```


A sudoku puzzle...  

```
 5 3 4 | 6 7 8 | 9 1 2 |
 6 7 2 | 1 9 5 | 3 4 8 |
 1 9 8 | 3 4 2 | 5 6 7 |
 - - -   - - -   - - -  
 8 5 9 | 7 6 1 | 4 2 3 |
 4 2 6 | 8 5 3 | 7 9 1 |
 7 1 3 | 9 2 4 | 8 5 6 |
 - - -   - - -   - - -  
 9 6 1 | 5 3 7 | 2 8 4 |
 2 8 7 | 4 1 9 | 6 3 5 |
 3 4 5 | 2 8 6 | 1 7 9 |
```

...and its solution numbers marked in red.
